Copywriting Project

My project focuses on creating a persuasive blog post in Italian to promote an app dedicated to buying and exchanging used clothes. Through the effective use of Cialdini's persuasion principles and careful structuring of content, I developed an engaging article that utilizes power words, spacing, and thoughtful formatting.

The entire process was managed using Google Document, integrating in-depth research conducted through SEMrush and citing sources to ensure reliability and authenticity in the message. The blog post aims to capture the attention of the target audience, increase awareness of the app, and promote concrete actions, contributing to the overall success of the marketing campaign.
